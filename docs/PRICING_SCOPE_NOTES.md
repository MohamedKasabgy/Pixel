# Pricing & Scope Notes

## Confirmed scope

- English-only website.
- WordPress + WooCommerce.
- 10–15 launch products.
- Product options: size, color, material, finish, paper type, quantity, notes.
- Customer customization and artwork upload.
- Customer login and client portal.
- Order tracking.
- Roles: customer, staff/employee, admin.
- Admin dashboard for products, orders, customers, quote requests, files, coupons.
- Coupons/discounts.
- Cart and wishlist.
- Around 300 weekly visitors/orders-related sessions initially.
- Brand identity exists.
- Domain/hosting exists.
- Max launch timeline: 3 weeks.

## Pricing-impact questions still needed

- Exact payment gateway required?
- Exact shipping carrier integrations required at launch or manual shipping only?
- Does pricing need live formula calculation for every product, or can WooCommerce variable products cover MVP?
- Are there proof approval steps before production?
- Should staff members have separate departments such as sales, pre-press, production, delivery?
- Does the client need Arabic later?
- Does the client need custom invoice design at launch?
- Is wishlist required at MVP or phase 2?

## Recommended phase split

### Phase 1 — Professional MVP

Visual website + WooCommerce + quote/upload + portal/tracking + admin basics.

### Phase 2 — Advanced Operations

Dynamic pricing matrix, proof approvals, production queue, shipping API integrations, staff department permissions, analytics dashboards.

### Phase 3 — Growth

Multilingual support, CRM integrations, marketing automation, advanced B2B pricing, company accounts.
